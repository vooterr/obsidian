# Comparing models for eyes classification 
## MobileNetV3Large
- Best version is `mobilenetv3l_lr5e4_p2g`
- Best epochs is 48-50 
- In average case the best variance. Better than efficentNet from alex reports on Adjit_data_split and full_nas2, but on Black Guy shows less effective result for closed eyes.
## EfficientNet
- Best versions is `efb0_newdata_p2a` and `efb0_128x64_lr5e4_p3c`
- Best epochs is 42 and 18 respectively
- For black_guy dataset efficient_shows better result
